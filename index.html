About Our Platform

Min Khant Kyaw

1st year IT Engineering Student

"This educational web platform named "CSAI edu" was developed in March, 2021, aiming for the children who weren't able to accomplish Computer Science class offered by Thate Pan Hub due to the political situations in Myanmar. I, as their teacher, wanted them to keep in touch with Computer Science, and in this way, I developed this, using Evolvo's Template (Only Frame) to save time. It took me at least 5 days to finish it as nearly eighty percent of this web platform were my works. I only added free, great & suitable courses from platforms like Google & edX. As this is my first experience of setting up an educational platform, I could only recommend free courses & add here, but I'll try to make this platform better as soon as possible."
